Why Choose Us

A nail experience built around you

We're not your average salon. Here's what makes a visit to Tee (DOT) Nail Bar different.

Every appointment is exclusively yours. No waiting, no rush — your nail tech is 100% focused on you and your nails throughout the entire session.
