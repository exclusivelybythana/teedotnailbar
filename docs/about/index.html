About

The story behind Tee (DOT) Nail Bar

Est. 2018

Hi, I'm Stephanie

Welcome to Tee (DOT) Nail Bar — a home-based nail salon in Sembawang, North Singapore, where every client gets my full, undivided attention.

I started this journey in 2018 out of a genuine love for nail art and the belief that great nails shouldn't be rushed or cookie-cutter. Since then, I've built a cosy studio at home, worked with hundreds of clients, and in 2025 launched a Studio arm for press-on nails and masterclasses.

What I do is 1-on-1, always. No shared slots, no rushing — just you, me, and your nails for the whole session.

I look forward to seeing you!
