Policies

Terms & Conditions

Please read these before booking. All policies are agreed upon at the point of deposit payment.

Heads up: These T&Cs are strictly enforced to keep the experience smooth for everyone. By paying your deposit, you confirm that you've read and agreed to everything below.

Deposit

  • A $20 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking.
  • The deposit offsets your final bill at the end of the appointment.
  • Deposit must be paid within 1 hour of receiving the confirmation message — otherwise your slot may be released.
  • The deposit is strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.

Cancellation

  • Cancellations are strictly not allowed once a deposit has been paid.
  • If you are unable to proceed, you may request a reschedule (see below) — but the deposit will not be returned regardless.

Reschedule

  • You may reschedule your appointment once only.
  • Reschedule requests must be made at least 3 days in advance.
  • Unforeseen circumstances (e.g. medical emergencies) are handled on a case-by-case basis — please reach out via WhatsApp.

Grace Period & Lateness

  • A grace period of 10 minutes applies to all appointments.
  • If you are more than 10 minutes late: your deposit is forfeited. The appointment may still proceed if there are no subsequent bookings, but this is not guaranteed.
  • If you are more than 15 minutes late: your appointment is cancelled and the deposit is forfeited.
  • If the service has to be simplified due to your lateness, the full original price will still be charged.
  • Please WhatsApp Tee immediately if you are running late.

House Rules

  • Cashless payment only — PayNow/PayLah/bank transfer. No cash accepted.
  • Strictly no companions — the space is a private home. Please come alone.
  • Message Tee when you reach the lift lobby before coming up to the unit.
  • Drinks are welcome; no food please.
  • Your nail art design must be confirmed with Tee at least 1 day before your appointment.
  • Promotions are non-stackable — only one promo or discount applies per visit.

Online Booking Flow

  • Check Instagram (@teedotnailbar) for open dates and times before booking on Fresha — Fresha has display limitations and bookings made outside of open slots will be cancelled.
  • After booking on Fresha, WhatsApp Tee within 1 hour to confirm — Fresha notifications can be unreliable.
  • Your appointment is only confirmed after deposit payment is made.
  • Festive-season slots (e.g. CNY, Christmas) use a separate Google Form — watch Instagram for the announcement.
