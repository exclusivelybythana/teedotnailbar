Welcome to Tee (DOT) Nail Bar: Your Premier Home-Based Nail Salon in Singapore

Are you looking for a top-notch nail salon in Singapore that offers affordable nail art and exceptional service? Look no further than Tee (DOT) Nail Bar! Located in the heart of Sembawang, we are proud to be recognised as one of the ThreeBestRated nail salons in the area. Whether you're in Yishun, Sembawang, Canberra, or anywhere in Singapore — our home-based nail salon is here to provide you with an unparalleled nail care experience.

Why Choose Tee (DOT) Nail Bar?

At Tee (DOT) Nail Bar, we believe that every visit should be a personalised experience. Here's what makes us different:

  • Expert Nail Technician: Our nail tech and business owner, Stephanie, has been dazzling clients with her nail artistry since 2018. If you count the early days when she was painting nails just for fun, her journey actually began back in 2015! Stephanie's passion for nail art is infectious, and she's dedicated to providing stunning results for every client.
  • Affordable Pricing: We offer a wide range of services — from classic manicures and pedicures to intricate nail art — all at competitive prices. Quality nail care doesn't have to break the bank!
  • Top-Quality Products: We use only the highest quality products to ensure your nails remain healthy and beautiful, with a wide variety of colours and finishes to suit every style.
  • Convenient Location: Our home-based salon in Sembawang is easily accessible for clients in Yishun and surrounding areas. Enjoy the comfort and privacy of a home setting while receiving professional nail care.
  • Personalised Service: We take the time to understand your preferences and tailor every service to meet your specific needs. Your satisfaction is our top priority!

Our Services

At Tee (DOT) Nail Bar, we offer a comprehensive range of nail services:

  • Manicures and Pedicures: Keep your hands and feet looking their best with our classic and spa treatments.
  • Nail Art: Express your creativity with custom nail art designs. From simple elegance to bold statements, we bring your vision to life.
  • Press-On Nails: Perfect for those who love to switch up their look — our custom press-on nails offer style and convenience.
  • Gel-X Extensions: Achieve the length and shape you desire with our durable gel-x nail extensions.
