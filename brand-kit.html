05 — Imagery
Brand Texture & Photography
Tee (DOT) Nail Bar
by Thana · since 2018
Hero Texture

Soft white feather / fur texture used as the primary hero background. Conveys warmth, softness, and luxury without colour distraction.

Photography Style

Bright, neutral-toned imagery with warm cream and white tones. Avoid harsh shadows or oversaturated colours — keep the palette soft and refined.

Overlay Approach

Semi-transparent cream overlay (#faf8f5 at 40–50% opacity) applied over imagery to keep text legible while preserving the background texture.
